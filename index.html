About us

The Rotaract Club of Mumbai Mulund South (RCMMS) is a youth(aged 16-30) wing of Rotary Club of Mumbai Mulund South. Rotaract Club of Mumbai Mulund South is one of the well-known and recognized clubs in the Rotary district 3141(Mumbai). This club has been highly active in various avenues such as society welfare activities, youth development activities, personal and professional development activities etc. It has been nominated for District level awards at various occasions.The club rating was 4th in the year 2015-16. RCMMS is the club with the young heart, average age of the members being between 16-30.

The club's theme for this year is -"Make a Difference(MAD) ". As per the theme, this year we aim to make a difference in society and also on a personal level. By our activities and events, we will try and reach to masses of people and make a positive impact in society. On an individual level, making a difference refers to gaining confidence, making friends, getting an exposure of outside world etc.
Rotaract Club of Mumbai Mulund South will help you to:

  • Develop professional and leadership skills.
  • Recognize the dignity and value of all useful occupations and opportunities to serve.
  • Recognize, practice and promote ethical standards as leadership qualities and vocational responsibilities.
  • Develop knowledge and understanding of the needs, problems and opportunities in the community and worldwide
  • Provide opportunities for personal and group activities to serve the community and promote international understanding and goodwill.
